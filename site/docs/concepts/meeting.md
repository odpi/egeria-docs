# Meeting

A *meeting* is a time when a group of people meet to discuss a topic. It is a type of [action](/concepts/action), which means the attendees, follow on actions, purpose, and cause can all be captured.

??? info "Further information"
    The *Meeting* open metadata type is described in [Model 0135 Actions For People](/types/1/0135-Actions-For-People).
